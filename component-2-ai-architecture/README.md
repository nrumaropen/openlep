## System Overview

OpenLEP integrates language access service data, compliance requirements, and outcome metrics into a standardized repository that supports AI-assisted compliance monitoring, risk identification, and decision-support dashboards for public-serving organizations.

### Data Inputs
- Organizations and programs
- Individual language needs
- Service events
- Language services delivered
- Complaints
- Satisfaction surveys

### AI-Assisted Analytics
- **Service Gap Detection** — identifies unmet language needs across programs and populations
- **Compliance Risk Scoring** — AI-assisted scoring tied to federal and state legal requirements
- **Complaint Pattern Analysis** — surfaces systemic service failures through complaint trend detection
- **Outcome Monitoring** — tracks service delivery outcomes over time
- **Predictive Compliance Alerts** — anticipates compliance risk before violations occur

### Dashboard Outputs
- Compliance Score
- Interpreter Utilization
- Language Demand Trends
- Unmet Service Requests
- Complaint Monitoring
